PREAMBLE

We, the student of Southwestern University, imploring the aid of Almighty God, in order to establish a Supreme Student Government that shall embody our ideals and aspirations, promote unity and understanding among us, uphold and protect our rights and promote our welfare, foster harmonious and beneficial relationship between us and other sectors of the University and society, instill in ourselves the spirit of nationalism and social responsibility, do ordain and promulgate this Constitution.
